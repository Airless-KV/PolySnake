PolySnake 
  This game is a modern, fully 3D reimagining of the classic arcade game
Snake. You control a continuously growing entity, collecting items and
avoiding deadly collisions with obstacles and your own tail. The twist?
You're no longer confined to a flat grid. Players must navigate the
surfaces of three-dimensional shapes like massive spheres, cubes, and
complex polyhedrons. It takes the familiar, addictive gameplay of
Snake and turns it into a 360-degree challenge that constantly tests
your spatial awareness.
